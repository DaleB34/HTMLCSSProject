This Socks

Have you ever done a load laundry and discovered that you lost the other half of your favorite pair of SpongeBob socks?

If so, I, Dale Bolido, with my extensive research on the loss of that piece of clothing you wear as a buffer between your foot and the shoe, will inform you of the reasons why they may be disappearing, and how to prevent it from happening.

Some may think that the phenomenon of losing socks is due entirely to human error and coincidence, while this may not be fully incorrect, the more likely answer to the mystery is that monsters, yes monsters, are making your beloved complements to footwear disappear.
